PIP: A social token.

About PIP

PIP is a social token that I created and deployed for the Solana blockchain. Solana's fast and cost-effective blockchain technology ensures that transactions involving PIP tokens happen seamlessly. This means quick and affordable interactions within the PIP community. While other tokens on the Solana blockchain are created with only real-world functionality in mind, PIP was created out of curiosity and a general desire to learn more about blockchain development. At the moment, PIP is exclusive to a short list of authorized holders. If you want to be added to the list of authorized holders and obtain some free PIP, contact me!

See PIP on Solscan

PIP's design is inspired by Frutiger Aero, the dominant design language of the tech world between 2004 and 2013.
