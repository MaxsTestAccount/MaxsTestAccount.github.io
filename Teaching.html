My teaching experience

I'm passionate about math education!

For over three years, I held the roles of Teaching Assistant, Corrector, and Proctor. As a Teaching Assistant, I held weekly directed discussion groups for various mathematics courses wherein I taught mathematical subjects ranging from Calculus to Discrete Mathematics for Computing. In each weekly session, I covered a list of practice problems based on the material that was taught that week. I always make a point of encouraging students to attempt the practice problems beforehand and bring their questions to the weekly discussion group. As George Pólya once said, "Mathematics, you see, is not a spectator sport."
